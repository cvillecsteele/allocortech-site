The Taurus AEX extends the Actuator Edition platform to higher voltages and currents, supporting 16S, 20S, and 24S battery configurations while maintaining the same isolated multi-protocol communication architecture. Its versatility beyond pure motor driving, including support for linear actuators and servos, makes it the standard choice for platforms requiring mixed actuation across a single, unified control architecture.

The Taurus AEX reduces thermal overhead by 20% in high-density motor controller installations compared to prior-generation designs, enabling tighter packaging in eVTOL booms, fuselages, and nacelles where thermal budget is as constrained as physical space.
