The Taurus C60 is the field-hardened variant of the Taurus motor controller family. Where the AE and AEX are optimized for integration into sealed vehicle structures, the C60 carries its own IP67 enclosure with ruggedized connectors, making it the right choice for exposed installations in maritime, ground vehicle, and harsh-environment aerospace applications.

At 6kW peak output in a 250-gram package with robust weather sealing, the C60 eliminates the design work of managing a separate protective housing, delivering the full Taurus FOC/SVPWM performance advantage directly to the airframe or hull mount.
