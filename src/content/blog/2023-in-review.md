2023 was a year of firsts. Across the Allocortech customer portfolio, six programs crossed milestones that their industries will be measuring themselves against for years. Here's what happened, and what we contributed.

## Elroy Air: World's First Turbogenerator-Hybrid Flight

Elroy Air flew the world's first turbogenerator-hybrid unmanned aircraft, with the Taurus 400V motor controller stack and Clio distributed power hub as part of the system that made it work. An 8-week MOTS integration from initial requirements to delivered hardware. The kind of timeline that changes what's possible for a startup on a compressed development schedule.

## Navier: N30 Pioneer Delivered to First Customer

Navier delivered its N30 electric hydrofoil to its first commercial customer, achieving 100nm range at 38¢/nm, a performance level that makes the economics of electric watercraft credible for the first time. The Dauphine vehicle management hub manages autodocking and active foil control. Navier's lead flight control engineer, Dr. Kenny Jensen, described getting the N30 "flying in less time than I've ever seen a similarly complex project take flight."

## Jump Aero: JA1 Pulse First Responder eVTOL Unveiled

Jump Aero unveiled the JA1 Pulse at EMS World Expo, a first-responder eVTOL designed to reach rural emergency scenes in 8 minutes. Falck placed the first order. USAF followed with $3.6M in funding. Clio Series B and Lohner standby batteries provide the vehicle's avionics backbone.

## Wing: 300,000+ Commercial Deliveries

Wing (Alphabet's drone delivery subsidiary) crossed 300,000 commercial deliveries and expanded operations to Walmart locations in Texas. Comet FTS ensures that their delivery UAS fleet has reliable flight termination capability at the scale of commercial operations.

## Electra.aero: World's First Hybrid eSTOL Flights

Electra.aero flew the EL-2 Goldfinch, the world's first hybrid eSTOL aircraft, achieving 150-foot ground roll takeoffs using blown-lift aerodynamics. A new category of aircraft performance, demonstrated.

## PteroDynamics: Transwing VTOL Maritime Demos

PteroDynamics closed a $7.5M seed round and conducted Transwing X-P4 maritime demonstrations for the US Navy with its folding-wing VTOL UAS. The platform demonstrated the kind of ship-deck operations that are changing what naval uncrewed systems can do.

Six programs. Six milestones that mattered. The common thread: aggressive timelines, demanding technical requirements, and customers who couldn't afford to wait for legacy suppliers.
